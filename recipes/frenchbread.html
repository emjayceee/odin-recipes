French Bread

Renowned for its crusty exterior, tender crumb, and unmatched flavor, French bread is often hailed as the pinnacle of bread craftsmanship.

Ingredients

  • Active dry yeast
  • Warm water
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Bread flour
  • Cornmeal

Directions

  1. Make the dough
  2. Knead the dough
  3. Form the loaves
  4. Let it rise
  5. Prep the loaves
  6. Bake the bread
